ABOUT

We combine the best engineering talent, world-class processes and cutting-edge technology expertise to lead in the design and development of high-quality products. Our domain expertise reduces development costs and time to quickly help our clients deliver innovative products that meet the unique challenges and requirements of their industries.

Connected Systems Partners provides full life cycle engineering services to the following vertical markets:

Communications

Networked access devices, wireline and wireless switching devices, transport and management devices

Consumer

Home multimedia infotainment systems and set top boxes, automotive electronics and infotainment systems, mobile hand held devices, automatic energy management devices

Industrial

Capital equipment manufacturing devices, robotics, industrial & motion controls devices, instrumentation, pumps, gauges and sensors, infrared sensors, heat sensors, and surveillance cameras

Medical

Medical diagnostic devices, monitoring devices, insulin pumps, active and passive monitoring devices
